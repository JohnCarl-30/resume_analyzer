# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Students and early-career job seekers who have a resume and a specific job post. They are often anxious, short on time, and unsure which keywords or phrasing matter. The job to be done is to check fit against that post and fix the gaps without leaving the product.

## Product Purpose

Resumae helps someone compare a resume to a real job description, see the match in plain language, and improve the resume in the same workspace. Success is a clearer match score, concrete gaps addressed, and a resume they can export or keep editing — not a dump of opaque AI suggestions.

## Positioning

AI lightly rewrites the resume for the job, with reviewable edits the user approves — not a black-box full rewrite.

## Brand Personality

Calm, clear, coach-like. The interface should feel like a patient review partner: steady guidance, short sentences, no hype. Confidence comes from clarity and control, not from loud marketing energy.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI SaaS: purple glow, gradient dashboards, “unleash your potential” copy
- Flashy career marketplaces: loud CTAs, badge spam, hustle / stock-photo vibes

## Design Principles

1. **Show the match, then the fix.** Analysis and editing stay one continuous path — never strand the user after a score.
2. **Light edits, human approval.** Propose changes; never silently overwrite the resume.
3. **Plain language over ATS jargon.** Name things the way a job seeker would say them.
4. **Quiet UI, loud document.** The resume layout is the hero; chrome stays out of the way.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Sensible defaults: visible keyboard focus, respect for reduced motion, solid text contrast. No formal WCAG target yet; raise the bar when a concrete compliance need appears.
